My name is Destiny, and I like to Tell Puns, Look at Pictures, and Bask in the Rain. This is some of my artworks during 2017-2018.Some of them I am proud of and some are not so great....But, nevertheless I hope you enjoy looking. :D

I named my gallery 'Basking in the Rain' because I wanted a sort of theme, and I thought this contradicting phrase would be perfect. And although rain may not always be the best, sometimes it's enjoyable if you just sit down listen and bask. In a way it's just like art, there are the good days and bad days, but if you sit down and let the creativity flow, you'll end up with a masterpiece.
